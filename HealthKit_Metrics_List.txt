IV. HKWorkout (Metrics related to workouts)

This section in HKSampleMapping.json defines mappings for HKWorkoutActivityType to FHIR codings.
Examples include: HKWorkoutActivityTypeAmericanFootball, HKWorkoutActivityTypeArchery, HKWorkoutActivityTypeBadminton, etc.
It does not list individual metric identifiers in the same way as Quantity or Category samples.

V. HKElectrocardiogram (Metrics related to ECGs)

This section defines mappings for HKElectrocardiogram classifications (e.g., sinusRhythm, atrialFibrillation)
and HKElectrocardiogramSymptomsStatus (e.g., present, notPresent).
It does not list distinct metric identifiers in the same way as Quantity or Category samples.
